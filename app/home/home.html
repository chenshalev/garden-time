It's Garden Time!

Welcome to the unique Garden App

that will help you maintain your private garden

Garden Time will provide alerts and collect information from caregivers in the garden.

By connecting to the Weather app and synchronizing with the calendar, you can enter tasks and alerts.

The caregivers in the garden will be required to report on the types of treatments done in the application, so that YOU, the owner of the garden can follow and intervene if necessary.
