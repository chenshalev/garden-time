Garden maintenance is a complex task that depends on different parameters. From the weather, plants suitable for the region, planting and fertilization regime, and the human factors that treat it. In a private home garden, installing a computerized watering system will not solve all maintenance and maintenance problems since it will operate mostly during the summer months. During the transitional seasons, and even in the winter, there may be dry weeks and busy garden owners are not always awake for additional watering. In addition, there are sometimes areas without a system, such as: potted plants, seasonal vegetable garden and more. Beyond the need to schedule the watering and determine the appropriate amount for each type and size of plant and wood, there are additional annual needs to remember to perform on time: fertilization, pruning, picking, planting and sowing.
